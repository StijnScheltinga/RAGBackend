# Silverthorne Legal Group – Employee Handbook (2025)

## 1. Working Hours and Expectations

Standard business hours are **8:30 AM to 5:30 PM, Monday through Friday**.  
Employees are expected to be available during core hours: **10:00 AM to 3:00 PM**.

Overtime for non-exempt employees must be approved in advance by a supervisor.

## 2. Leave Policies

### 2.1 Annual Leave

Full-time employees receive **20 days of paid annual leave** per calendar year.  
A maximum of **5 unused days may be carried over** to the next year.

Annual leave requests must be submitted at least **7 days in advance**.

### 2.2 Sick Leave

Employees receive **8 paid sick days** per year.  
Absences longer than **2 consecutive days** require a doctor’s note.

Unused sick leave does **not** carry over.

### 2.3 Court Leave

Employees summoned for jury duty or subpoenaed as a witness will receive paid court leave for the duration of their required service.

## 3. Professional Development

SLG offers an annual **$1,500 Continuing Legal Education (CLE) budget** for each attorney.  
Unused CLE funds do not carry over.

## 4. Performance Reviews

All employees receive:
- A probationary review at 90 days.
- Annual performance reviews in January.

Reviews evaluate:
- Professional competence.
- Client communication.
- Adherence to deadlines.
- Team collaboration.

## 5. Code of Conduct

Employees must:
- Maintain client confidentiality.
- Avoid conflicts of interest.
- Report ethical concerns to the Ethics Committee.
- Treat colleagues and clients with respect at all times.

Violations may result in disciplinary action, including termination.
